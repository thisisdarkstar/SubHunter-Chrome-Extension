About

SubHunter v1.0.0

A free, open-source subdomain enumeration tool for Chrome.

Features:
• Certificate Transparency log queries (crt.sh)
• Certspotter passive DNS
• DNS-over-HTTPS validation
• Customizable brute-force scanning
• Export to TXT, JSON, or CSV

Disclaimer: This tool is for authorized security testing and educational purposes only. Always ensure you have permission before scanning any domain.
